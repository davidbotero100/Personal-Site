    LICAD

    Large Industrial & Civil Autonomous Design (LICAD) is a system that
    automates the design of civil infrastructure; from roads, subdivisions, bridges
    to residential buildings and factories. With planed features for vastly more complex structures,
    the project stems from a deep drive of contributing to the design and construction of infrastructure
    on a multi-planetary level for carbon based life and machines. Demo coming soon.



    The Grassroots Farm Woodfired Pizza App

    A cross-platform mobile app for the family-owned Grassroots Farm Woodfired Pizza business in Lucknow, ON.

    Function: Employee hour tracking, integrates with Wave and Square's APIs
    Purpose: Saves time for staff and management can easily review employee hours before releasing payments

    Function: Track ingredients used per pizza batch and stores each batch got sold to
    Purpose: Pull only affected inventory from stores in case of ingredient recall, minimizing product loss
