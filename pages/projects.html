    LICAD

    Large Industrial & Civil Autonomous Design (LICAD) is a system that automates the design of civil infrastructure;
    from roads, subdivisions, bridges to residential buildings, warehouses, and factories.

    With planed features for vastly more complex structures and physical constraints, the project stems from a deep drive of contributing
    to the design and construction of infrastructure on a multi-planetary level for carbon-based-life and machines.

    LICAD is part-one of a three-part project. Part-two is to redesign construction materials,
    along with their manufacturing processes, with part-three in mind; automate the construction of infrastructure.

    Earthly construction materials and practices are not fit for multiplanetry expansion.
    Can you imagine shipping construction materials to the moon?
    If the goal is to build the most expensive building in the solar system, great.



    LICAD demo coming soon.




    The Grassroots Farm Woodfired Pizza App

    A cross-platform mobile app for the family-owned Grassroots Farm Woodfired Pizza business in Lucknow, ON.

    Function: Employee hour tracking, integrates with Wave and Square's APIs.
    Benefits: Saves time for staff, while management can quickly review hours before processing payments.

    Function: Track ingredients used per pizza batch and records the store each batch was sold to
    Benefits: In the event of an ingredient recall, only the affected inventory is pulled from stores, reducing product loss.
