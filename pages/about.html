About Me


David is a recent (December 2024) Computer Science graduate and former Civil Engineering Technologist.
Originally from Medellin, Colombia, David has spent just over half of his life in Canada - Ontario and British Columbia.

Spending most of his time developing personal projects and building products for friends and family, as a contractor,
he thoroughly enjoys the entire system design process. From understanding a process and abstracting away
complex ideas -> to networking with users and stake holders -> to designing, implementing, and deploying the system.

David is also a passionate DownHill and FreeRide mountain biker, extremely fortunate to ride
most of the world's best riding spots with great company.

Other than these, David is just another human who aims to attain fundamental understanding
to be able to contribute useful and helpful ideas for the long run, and relative benefit, to this
expression of the universe that we are.
